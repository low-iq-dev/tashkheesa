Frequently asked questions

What files can I upload?

PDFs, JPG/PNG images and DICOM files (as .zip). If you’re not sure, upload what you have and our team will guide you.

How long does it take to receive my report?

Standard cases are delivered within 72 hours. Fast-track and Premium cases are targeted within 24 hours.

Is Tashkheesa replacing my main doctor?

No. We provide a second opinion to help you make better decisions – your primary doctor remains responsible for your ongoing care.

Is my data private?

Your files are stored securely and only shared with the specialists who need them to prepare your report.
