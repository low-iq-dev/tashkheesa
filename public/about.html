Our Story

Built from a Simple Belief

Every patient deserves clarity before making critical medical decisions.

Why We Started

Tashkheesa was born from a simple observation: patients facing critical diagnoses often lack access to specialist second opinions. We set out to bridge that gap by connecting patients with hospital-based specialists through a secure, structured review process.

Our platform transforms complex medical files into clear, actionable specialist reports that help patients and their families make informed decisions about their care.

How We're Different

Unlike generic telehealth platforms, Tashkheesa focuses exclusively on structured medical second opinions. Every case is reviewed by a hospital-based specialist with domain expertise, and every report follows a clear format designed for practical decision-making.

Our 24-72 hour turnaround ensures patients get the clarity they need without unnecessary delays when time matters most.
