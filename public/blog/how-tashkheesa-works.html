Get a Medical Second Opinion in 3 Simple Steps

By Tashkheesa Medical Team · March 15, 2026 · 5 min read

Getting a medical second opinion used to mean booking another appointment, traveling to a different hospital, waiting weeks, and paying for another full consultation. Not anymore. Tashkheesa brings the specialist to you — digitally, securely, and within 24-72 hours.

The 3-Step Process

1

Upload Your Medical Records

Securely upload your medical scans, lab results, pathology reports, imaging, or any relevant documents. Our AI validation system checks that your files are genuine medical documents and are clear enough for specialist review. The entire upload process takes less than 5 minutes.

2

A Specialist Reviews Your Case

Your case is assigned to a board-certified specialist in your condition's specific field. Whether it's cardiology, neurology, oncology, or any of our 16+ specialties, the reviewing doctor examines everything thoroughly — your imaging, lab work, clinical history, and current treatment plan.

3

Receive Your Detailed Report

Within 24-72 hours, you receive a comprehensive bilingual report (Arabic and English) that includes the specialist's findings, diagnosis confirmation or revision, treatment recommendations, and next steps. Plus, you get free follow-up messaging with your reviewing doctor for any questions.

What's Included in Every Report

  • Full specialist case review — your entire medical file examined by an expert
  • Detailed written analysis — not a one-line summary, a thorough breakdown
  • Diagnosis confirmation or revision — did the first doctor get it right?
  • Treatment recommendations — what the specialist would suggest
  • Bilingual report — both Arabic and English so you understand everything
  • Free follow-up — message your doctor after the report at no extra cost
