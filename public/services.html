Radiology & Imaging

Second opinions on CT, MRI, X-ray and ultrasound imaging from sub-specialist radiologists.

CT scans

Head, chest, abdomen, pelvis and spine CT scans for trauma, tumors, infection and follow-up.

MRI scans

Brain, spine, joints and organ MRI for neurological, orthopedic and abdominal conditions.

Chest X-ray

Chest X-ray review for infection, fluid, chronic lung disease, heart size and post-operative changes.

Ultrasound (image review)

Abdominal, pelvic, thyroid and soft-tissue ultrasound image reports.

Neuro-imaging

Brain and spine CT/MRI for stroke, tumors, demyelination and degenerative disease.

Oncology imaging

Staging and follow-up CT/MRI/PET-CT for solid tumors and lymphomas.

Imaging facilities

All imaging opinions are based on scans performed at Shifa Hospital or other accredited centers.

Cardiology

Second opinions on heart tests, ECGs, echocardiograms and cardiac imaging from sub-specialist cardiologists.

ECG / Rhythm review

Interpretation of ECGs and Holter monitoring for arrhythmias, conduction problems and ischemia.

Echocardiogram review

Second look at echo studies for valve disease, heart function, cardiomyopathy and structural abnormalities.

Stress tests & perfusion scans

Review of treadmill or pharmacologic stress tests and perfusion imaging for coronary artery disease.

Cardiac CT / MRI

Opinion on calcium scoring, CT coronary angiography and cardiac MRI for function, scar and viability.

Cardiology facilities

Cardiology cases are reviewed using ECGs, echo studies and cardiac imaging performed at Shifa Hospital.

Oncology

Second opinions on cancer diagnosis, staging and treatment plans from expert oncologists.

Pathology report review

Re-reading biopsy and surgical pathology reports to confirm tumor type, grade and margins.

PET / CT oncology imaging

Expert review of PET/CT and CT scans for staging, response assessment and recurrence detection.

Treatment plan review

Second opinion on chemotherapy, targeted therapy and radiotherapy plans.

Tumor board consultation

Multi-disciplinary evaluation including radiologists, oncologists, and surgeons.

Oncology facilities

Oncology opinions integrate Shifa Hospital’s imaging, pathology and oncology expertise.

Neurology

Second opinions on brain MRI, spine imaging, EEG/EMG reports and neurological conditions.

Brain MRI / CT

Review for stroke, tumors, demyelinating disease, seizures and chronic headaches.

Spine MRI

Assessment of cervical, thoracic and lumbar MRI for disc disease, stenosis and cord compression.

EEG / EMG studies

Second opinion on EEG and nerve conduction/EMG tests for epilepsy and neuromuscular disease.

Neuro-imaging packages

Combined brain and spine imaging review with neurological interpretation.

Neurology facilities

Neurology opinions use imaging, EEG/EMG and clinical assessments from Shifa Hospital’s neuro team.

Lab & Pathology

Second opinions on blood tests, tumor markers, biopsy reports and genetic testing.

Blood tests & biochemistry

Review of CBC, kidney, liver, thyroid and metabolic panels with clinical interpretation.

Tumor markers

Interpretation of cancer-related blood markers and their clinical significance.

Histopathology

Detailed second read of biopsy and surgical pathology by senior pathologists.

Genetic & molecular tests

Review of molecular profiling, IHC panels, and genetic markers relevant to treatment.

Lab & pathology facilities

Lab opinions and pathology reviews are based on accredited testing performed in Shifa Hospital’s laboratory.
