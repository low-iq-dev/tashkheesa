How your second opinion is prepared

Every report goes through a clear process so you know exactly what you’re getting – and who is responsible for it.

  1. Your files are reviewed for quality and completeness by our coordination team.
  2. The case is assigned to a consultant-level specialist with the right sub-specialty.
  3. The doctor reviews your imaging, lab results and clinical background together.
  4. A structured written report is prepared with findings, interpretation and next-step advice.
  5. The report is checked for clarity, then delivered to you via the Tashkheesa portal.
